Assignments

Assignment 1: Research Proposal

First, I completed a research proposal briefly outlining the research project. The proposal addresses the plan of work, the reserach areas implicated, the data that would be collected, and the presentation of the research project.

Assignment 2: Environmental Scan

Second, I completed an environmental scan that engages more deeply into the rhetoric and DH literature to suggest how my project would contribute. This scan provides the most complete vision of the project, including similar projects as examples.

Assignment 3: Prototypes

Third, I completed a series of protoypes to begin experimenting with the project. The reflection details why I chose to make 2 TEIs and 1 map. My wireframe shows what I imagine the final research website looking like. I also included metadata to show the logisitics of project management.

Finally, I completed a draft grant proposal to suggest how much the project proposed in the research proposal and environmental scan would complete.
